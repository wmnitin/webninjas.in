About Us

At Webninjas, we specialize in providing placement-focused Frontend Bootcamps designed for students, intermediate coders, career switchers, and aspiring developers. Whether you're a student looking to build a strong foundation, an intermediate coder aiming to master the latest tools, or a professional wanting to switch careers, our bootcamps are tailored to help you achieve your goals.

We focus on the latest technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, ReactJS, NextJS, and DevOps, offering practical, hands-on training to prepare you for real-world challenges. Our training process includes resume building, interview preparation, LinkedIn & GitHub profile optimization, job application support, and dedicated placement assistance.

Our live classes are interactive and designed to enhance learning efficiency with minimal theory and maximum practical knowledge. We provide downloadable recordings, certificates of completion, and continuous guidance until you secure your first job. With AI tools like ChatGPT and DeepSeek integrated into the learning process, we ensure you stay ahead of the competition in the evolving tech landscape.
